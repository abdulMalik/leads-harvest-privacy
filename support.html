Leads Harvest — Support

Need help, found a bug, or have a feature request?

Email leadsharvestsupport@gmail.com — we usually reply within 24 hours.

When reporting a bug, include:

  • Chrome version (chrome://version)
  • Leads Harvest version (chrome://extensions, expand Leads Harvest)
  • The search query you were harvesting
  • What happened vs. what you expected
